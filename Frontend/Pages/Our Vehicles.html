Lamborghini URUS

The Lamborghini Urus is a powerful and luxurious SUV, blending supercar performance with everyday practicality. Powered by a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine, it produces 650 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque, allowing it to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds. The Urus offers advanced driving modes, including off-road capabilities, and features a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. Inside, it boasts a premium interior with cutting-edge technology like a touchscreen infotainment system, customizable digital displays, and top-notch materials. The Lamborghini Urus in the UAE is priced at approximately AED 997,500.

Porsche Taycan

The 2024 Porsche Taycan is an all-electric sports sedan offering multiple trims, including the base Taycan, 4S, GTS, Turbo, and Turbo S. It delivers impressive performance, with the Turbo S model accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.6 seconds. The Taycan features quick DC fast-charging capabilities and provides a driving experience characteristic of Porsche's engineering excellence (375,000).

It balances rugged capability with opulent comfort, catering to adventurers and luxury enthusiasts alike(535,000 AED).

Land Rover Defender

The Land Rover Defender is a rugged, versatile SUV designed for both off-road capability and on-road luxury. Available in multiple configurations, it features a choice of engines, including a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder and a 3.0L supercharged inline-six, delivering up to 395 horsepower. The Defender is known for its exceptional off-road prowess, with advanced terrain response systems, all-wheel drive, and a high ground clearance. Inside, it offers a modern yet durable cabin with seating for up to 7 passengers, premium materials, and advanced tech features like a touchscreen infotainment system and driver assistance systems. The Defender’s price in the UAE starts at approximately AED 240,000, depending on the model and specifications..

Nissan Patrol 2025

The 2023 Nissan Patrol is a powerful and versatile SUV designed to tackle both urban streets and rugged terrains. It offers two engine options: a 3.8L V6 engine with 235 kW and 386 Nm of torque or a 3.5L twin-turbo V6 producing 317 kW and 700 Nm of torque. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, ensuring smooth performance. The Patrol's spacious interior includes premium materials, advanced tech features like NissanConnect 2.0, ProPILOT assistance, and a Klipsch Premium Audio System. With its adaptive air suspension and enhanced off-road capability, the Patrol is ideal for adventure. Prices start around AED 250,000.

Buggati Chiron Sport

The Bugatti Chiron Sport is an extraordinary hypercar that combines luxury, speed, and cutting-edge technology. Powered by an 8.0L quad-turbocharged W16 engine, it delivers an astonishing 1,479 horsepower and 1,600 Nm of torque. Jetour T2

Nissan GTR

The Nissan GT-R is an iconic high-performance sports car known for its blend of power, agility, and advanced technology. Powered by a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V6 engine, it delivers up to 565 horsepower and 633 Nm of torque, paired with a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission for lightning-fast gear shifts. Renowned for its impressive acceleration, the GT-R can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds. Its advanced all-wheel-drive system and adaptive suspension provide exceptional handling and stability, both on the track and the road. Inside, the GT-R features a driver-focused cockpit with premium materials, a touchscreen infotainment system, and various comfort amenities. The price of the Nissan GT-R in the UAE starts at approximately AED 498,500.
